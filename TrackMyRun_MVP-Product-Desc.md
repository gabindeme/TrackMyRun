## TrackMyRun – MVP Product Description

### Product Overview

**TrackMyRun** is a web application that connects to the **Strava API** to provide runners and endurance athletes with a **clean, insightful analytics dashboard** and a **“Year in Sport” experience inspired by Spotify Wrapped**.

The app focuses on **personal performance insights and visual storytelling**, offering premium-style statistics without requiring a Strava subscription.

---

## Core Value Proposition

TrackMyRun turns raw Strava data into:

* **Clear performance insights**
* **Beautiful visual dashboards**
* **A shareable yearly recap of your sport activity**

All in one simple, customizable interface.

---

## MVP Key Features

### 1. Strava Connection

* Secure OAuth login with Strava
* Read-only access to activities
* Manual data refresh (MVP scope)

---

### 2. Main Analytics Dashboard

* Total distance, time, elevation gain
* Weekly, monthly, yearly summaries
* Activity breakdown by sport (run, ride, others)
* Average pace, speed, heart rate (when available)

---

### 3. Performance Trends

* Distance and volume progression over time
* Pace / speed evolution
* Heart rate trends and training load (basic)
* Personal bests (fastest 5K, 10K, longest run, etc.)

---

### 4. “Year in Sport” (Spotify Wrapped–Style)

A dedicated **end-of-year experience** with:

* Total distance, time, and elevation for the year
* Number of activities completed
* Most active month and week
* Longest run / ride
* Fastest pace achieved
* Favorite day of the week
* Fun insights (e.g. *“You ran the equivalent of crossing France twice”*)
* Animated slides or cards
* Shareable images or stories (social-ready)

---

### 5. Data Visualization & UI

* Interactive charts and graphs
* Clean, minimal, modern design
* Mobile-friendly layout
* Focus on storytelling, not social competition

---

## MVP Technical Scope

### Frontend and Backend

* Same stack as the boilerplate

---

## Out of Scope (Post-MVP)

* Training plans & coaching
* Live tracking
* Social feed
* Multi-user comparison
* AI-generated training advice

---

## MVP Success Criteria

* User connects Strava in < 2 minutes
* Dashboard loads quickly with cached data
* “Year in Sport” recap is visually engaging
* Users can export and share their recap
* Clear alternative to Strava Premium for analytics
